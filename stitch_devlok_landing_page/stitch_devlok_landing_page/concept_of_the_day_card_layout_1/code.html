PHILOSOPHY

Prakṛti

"The Primal Creative Force"

In the vast tapestry of Vedic thought, Prakṛti is the fundamental matrix of the universe. It is nature in its unmanifested state—the equilibrium of the three Gunas (Sattva, Rajas, and Tamas).

Unlike the static consciousness of Purusha, Prakṛti is dynamic, ever-changing, and the source of all material form. It is the celestial womb from which the dance of existence emerges, transforming silence into the symphony of the cosmos.
